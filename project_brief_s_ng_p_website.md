# PROJECT BRIEF: Sống Đẹp – Gương Bác Trong Thời Đại Số

## 1. Project Overview
- **Course:** HCM202 – Ho Chi Minh Ideology
- **Format:** Interactive Multi-page Website
- **Central Theme:** "Ở đời và làm người phải như thế nào?" (How to live and be human?)
- **Target Audience:** Gen Z Students in the digital age.

## 2. Design Philosophy: "Modern Vietnamese Heritage"
- **Visual Identity:** A blend of traditional Vietnamese aesthetics (Dong Son drum patterns, lotus motifs, "Giấy Dó" paper texture) with modern digital UI elements (clean typography, glassmorphism, AI-themed illustrations).
- **Color Palette:**
    - Primary: #da251d (Revolutionary Red)
    - Surface: #fdf9eb (Traditional Paper Cream)
    - Accents: Gold and Earthy tones.
- **Typography:** LIBRE_CASLON_TEXT for headings (traditional, authoritative yet elegant) and clean sans-serif for body copy.

## 3. Site Structure & Key Pages
1. **Triết Lý Đạo Đức Bác Hồ (Philosophy):** Defines the "Root" of morality and visualizes the 5 moral standards (Cần, Kiệm, Liêm, Chính, Chí công vô tư).
2. **Tấm Gương Bác Hồ (Case Studies):** 5 storytelling cards illustrating Uncle Ho's life with lessons for modern youth.
3. **Bạn Đang Sống Thế Nào? (Interactive Quiz):** 10 real-world scenarios for students to self-reflect on their ethics in the AI era.
4. **Thực Tiễn Hôm Nay (Modern Context):** Deep analysis of AI ethics, social media behavior, individualism, and anti-corruption.
5. **Tôi Chọn Cách Sống (Conclusion):** A manifesto summarizing the project's answer to the central question (CQ9.5).
6. **Minh Bạch Công Nghệ AI (AI Usage Report):** Transparency on how generative AI was used to assist research and design.

## 4. Visual Assets
- **Illustrations:** Custom "Digital Silk Painting" style imagery showing students collaborating with AI under the guidance of traditional values.
- **Backgrounds:** Subtle Dong Son drum watermark to establish cultural grounding.
- **Icons:** Traditional motifs (Lotus, Books, Tools) rendered with clean, modern lines.

## 5. Technology & Tools
- **Research/Content:** ChatGPT (logic drafting), Original Course Documents.
- **Design:** Stitch AI (UI/UX Design), Canva AI (Infographics).
- **Framework:** HTML5/Tailwind CSS with Modern Vietnamese Heritage custom design system.
